Project One

Tower of Hanoi

A project writted in Java, using openGL and the puzzle Tower of Hanoi.

Once run the program, press Space will show what's next right solution in the puzzle. Press A will show you the whole solutions and it will automatically restart from the first step if the solution goes to the end. Press D to switch back to the step-by-step mode.

Project Two

Music Editor

Music Editor is a basic MVC model project written in Java. It enables View synchronizes with the sound and enables user to either play or revise the music.

Press P to play the music. Press SPACE to pause and resume. Mouse click one Note on the program screen: 1. Press A to add a new Note; 2. Press I revise the instrument; 3. Press R will remove the Note; 4. Press M and don’t release, click mouse and drag it to the place you want can move the Note; 5. Press V to revise volume; 6. Press D and press mouse not release and drag it to the right side to add more length to it.

Project Three

Forbidden Island

Forbidden Island is a game done with a partner using JAVA.

A pilot suffered airplane crash and fells on the island. He needs to pick all this stuff then get to the place where helicopter is. He need to finish these steps in limited time because of risng tide. The island will be submerged by the sea. Use arrow Key for moving pilot and once you get to the stuff you can pick up stuff with you. Once pick up all stuff and get to the helicopter before sea floods the island, you will survive and win the game.

Three modes for this game. Press Key to change game mode. M for normal island; T for terrain island; R for diamond mountain island.
